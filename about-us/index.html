Here are three
basic principles
which we use
to create truly ergonomic
things
01. INNOVATIVE HANDLES

According to our customers, many brands do not pay attention to the comfort of the handles.

DEISS knows that you need a convenient handle to mix thick porridge, turn a large heavy steak or peel a hard pear. Our handles will greatly facilitate the process. That is why we not only create comfortable ergonomic handles for each tool.

We also update them from time to time thanks toreviews of our customers 💛

02. NON-SLIP &
NON-SCRATCH

It is also very important for DEISS that your tools get along well with any cutlery. To do this, we make our non-scratch tools so that you can even grate the cheese right in any plate.

DEISS products that interact with the surface of a table or cutting board are also non-slip. This allows us to use less effort to maintain balance and focus on the process more.

03. Multi -
functionality

Most DEISS employees know what it's like when they don't have enough time to cook and do the dishes. Therefore, it was important for us to develop a product that will create delicious meals faster than delivery arrives. That is why most of our tools are multifunctional or multi-tasking: use several tools and wash one :) In addition, we made sure that they were all extremely easy to clean.

And for quick recipes, our chef has created a special section in his blog. Follow the link and enjoy cooking 💛
